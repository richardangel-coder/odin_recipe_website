Over Night Oats

Breakfast Over Night blueberry cheesecake Oats

This is a very high protein over night oats breakfast recipe

made with whole foods

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats
  • Chai seeds
  • Milk of your choice
  • Greek yogurt
  • Raw honey
  • blueberry
  • Banana

Recipe instructions

  1. add 50g of rolled oats into mason jar
  2. add 2 table spoons of plain greek yogurt
  3. add half a cup of milk or milk of choice
  4. add 1 table spoon of raw honey
  5. add a hand full of blueberries and one sliced banana
