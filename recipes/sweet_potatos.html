Over Night Oats

summer sweet potatoes

This is an amazing side to your summer dinners

Unique sweet potato recipe thats simple and delicious

Ingredients

  • 1 whole sweet potato
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper
  • cinnamon

Recipe instructions

  1. chop whjole sweet potato into small to medium sized pieces
  2. add chopped potatos into an oven pan
  3. add olive oil till all pieces are covered eveanly
  4. add salt and pepper to taste
  5. dust cinnamon evenly over all pieces
  6. give the potatoes a nice toss in the pan
  7. put into oven for 40mins and 400 degree F
