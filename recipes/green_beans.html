Green Beans

Spring/summer green beans

This is a very nice summer/spring recipe of green beans

Thes zesty green beans are great to pair with all summer dishes

Ingredients

  • Green beans
  • olive oil
  • Garlic
  • chili pepper flakes
  • salt and pepper
  • lemon

Recipe instructions

  1. finely chop 6 cloves of garlic
  2. add the chopped garlic and 1 table spoon of chili flakes into a well oiled sauce pan on medium heat
  3. after washing your bag of green beans add them to the sauce pan when the garlic is lightly browned
  4. let the green beans cook for 2-4 minutes and then add salt and pepper to taste
  5. finally add 1 table spoon of lemon juice to pan while sturring
